Austin Breakfast Tacos

Prep Time=10 min Cook Time=35 min Total Time=45 min

These tacos are very good, make them.

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices uncoocked thick-cut bacon
  • 3 tbsp(42g) unsalted butter
  • 8-10 large eggs
  • 8 taco-sized flour tortillas
  • 1/3 cup (42g) crumbled cotija cheese
  • Jalapeno salsa

Directions

  1. Line a baking sheet with foil,
    and arrange the bacon in an even layer on top.
    Place in a cold oven, and set the oven to 420°F(220°C).
    Roast the bacon for 20 minutes or until nicley browned,
    crispy, and cooked through. Remove the bacon, and let it
    drain and cool on the paper towels.

  2. In a large nonstick skillet,
    heat the butter over medium heat.
    In a large bowl, whisk the eggs
    until thoroughly combined.
    Once the butter is melted and bubbling,
    add the eggs to the skillet.

  3. Constantly stir the eggs, scrambling
    together the cooked egg with the uncooked egg.
    Just before the eggs are fully cooked,
    remove the skillet from the heat.
    Countinue stirring until the eggs are
    just barley set together and cooked but not rubbery.
    Season to taste with salt and pepper.

  4. If the tortillas arent already charred
    from making them first from scrath,
    heat a medium skillet over medium-high heat.
    Once hot, working 1 at a time,
    very lightly cchar the tortillas on both sides.
    If the pan is hot enough, this should
    take 20 to 25 seconds per side. Turn the
    heat down if theyre charring too quickly. If the tortillas
    are already charred, make sure theyre hot a pliable.

  5. To assemble the tacos, In the cnter of each
    charred tortilla, add a scoop of the scrambled eggs,
    followed by a strip of cooked bacon, crumbled
    cotiha, cilantro leaves, and jalapeno salsa
    Fold as a taco, and serve immediatley with more salsa.
